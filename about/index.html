About Us

About

Murray Surgical is a AAAHC-accredited ambulatory surgery center, offering a hospital-like setting for safe and effective treatment in restorative dental procedures. Our center is conveniently located in Murray, Utah treating hundreds of children each year. Our team of skilled providers includes board-certified pediatric dentists, board-certified CRNA’s, and highly trained registered nurses to ensure safety of our young patients and that the detail of the treatment plan is followed through.

What Does AAAHC Mean?

AAAHC, or the Accreditation Association for Ambulatory Health Care, is a private, non-profit organization formed in 1979. Our center has gone through independent reviews of our whole organization to make sure our policies, procedures, processes and outcomes deliver safe, high-quality patient care and meet nationally-recognized standards. AAAHC routinely reviews, revises Standards, measures performance, and provides consultation and education. With the anticipation of an upcoming surgery, not only are the physician’s credentials important but also the practice as a whole. Go to a place that offers safe, high-quality care.

Providing Sedation For Peace of Mind

Going into surgery can be a scary thought, especially for your little ones. You may be thinking, why does my child need to be under anesthesia for dental work? There are many answers to this question. Sedation provides a less tragic experience and allows the doctor to complete all of the work safely. These means no more follow up visits to complete the treatment plan. The child will have no recollection of the visit, making it easier to go back to the dentist office for regular checkups. Our center provides a comfortable environment that puts children - and parents - at ease during the treatment.
Our center is the best option when complex restorative work is not possible in a traditional dentist office. It can become dangerous with an uncooperative patient or unrealistic for the child to sit still for extended periods of time. Coming to our center allows the dentist to focus on the restorative treatments while our anesthesia provider continuously monitors the patient while under sedation. This approach offers the safest, most comfortable, and most expertly rendered approach to dental care.
