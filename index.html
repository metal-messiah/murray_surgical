Why is an AAAHC Accredited Facility a Better Choice for Patients and Doctors?

The AAAHC certificate of accreditation is a sign that a Murray Surgical meets or exceeds nationally-recognized standards and is widely recognized as a symbol of quality by third party payers, medical organizations, liability insurance companies, state and federal agencies and the public.

AAAHC accreditation means that Murray Surgical participates in self-evaluation, peer review and education to continuously improve its care and services. We also commit to a thorough, on-site survey at least every three years.
